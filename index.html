My fascination with geospatial technology began while exploring its potential to address critical environmental issues. Through hands-on projects, including an interactive map with King County Metro Geospatial data, I've honed my skills in QGIS, Python, Geopandas, Folium, Javascript, HTML, and CSS. These tools have empowered me to transform data into actionable insights, showcasing the power of geospatial analysis in making a positive impact on our planet. Beyond my technical expertise, I thrive on connecting with people and sharing knowledge. My experience as a community organizer has equipped me with the unique ability to engage communities and foster collaborations that drive social change. Now, as I transition into the geospatial field, I aim to merge these skills to advocate for environmental sustainability and conservation. This website is a portfolio of my work, a testament to my journey, and a platform to share insights and discoveries in the field of geospatial analysis. Join me as I explore the intersections of data, technology, and environmental stewardship.
